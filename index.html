What's New

A log of every fix and feature added to PackRip.

v14 — Bug Fixes + New Features
Current version
FIXEX, GX, V, VMAX, VSTAR and full-art cards can no longer appear in pack slots 1–8. They are strictly locked to the last 2 hit slots only.
FIXHolographic sheen now correctly shows on EX, GX, V, VMAX, VSTAR and all hit-rarity cards. The card face was missing position:relative which was hiding all shimmer effects.
NEWDaily Challenge — a new featured set every day. Open 3 packs from it to earn bonus Pack Points. Resets at midnight.
NEWDaily Streak — open packs every day to build a streak. 3+ days earns a 10% Pack Points bonus, 7+ days earns 25%.
NEWLucky Card — one random card per pack is crowned with a ✨ sparkle. It earns double sell value.
NEWThis Changelog screen so you always know what changed.
v13 — Hints + Shiny Overhaul
Previous version
FIXTeam Rocket binder — removed wrong aliases that let any Blastoise/Charizard fill a "Dark" slot. Only actual "Dark [Pokémon]" named cards count now.
FIXBinder pick-mode hints now show the full correct slot name (e.g. "Dark Blastoise") instead of just the base Pokémon name.
IMPROVEAll holo/ultra/secret/rev-holo sheen effects completely rebuilt with multi-layer prismatic gradients — each rarity tier has its own distinct animated shimmer.
v12 — Team Rocket Fix
FIXTeam Rocket "Dark Pokémon" binder slots had incorrect plain-name aliases making any matching Pokémon valid. Fixed so only cards literally named "Dark [Pokémon]" count.
v11 — Major Feature Update
NEWSet Dex — browse every card in any set alphabetically with rarity filters and search.
NEWPack Store — sell duplicate cards for Pack Points and spend 150 pts on a God Pack from any set you choose.
NEWBinder completion reward — finishing a challenge binder awards a God Pack from a random set.
NEWName-locked binder slots — challenge binder slots only accept the correct Pokémon (any version/rarity of that name).
NEWCard tilt — click and hold enlarged cards to tilt them with a 3D parallax effect.
NEWSet Finder — tapping an empty binder slot with no matching cards shows which sets to open to find that Pokémon.
NEWEeveelutions binder ex-slots now also accept GX, V, VMAX, and VSTAR variants.
IMPROVECustom binder creation with colour and icon selection.
v6–v10 — Core Game
NEWPack simulator with slide-to-open mechanic, 1-pack and 10-pack modes.
NEWSwipe card viewer — flip through cards one at a time or reveal all at once.
NEWCollection — track every card you've pulled with rarity filters and search.
NEWBinder Challenges — structured fill-the-binder goals with 15+ themed challenges.
NEWGod Pack (1/500 chance) — all 10 cards are hit-rarity.
NEWMystery Pack, Sylveon Fan Pack, Eevee Grove, and Cozy Creatures special sets.
IMPROVEEX/GX/V/VMAX/VSTAR cards correctly treated as hit-tier and restricted to slots 9–10.
